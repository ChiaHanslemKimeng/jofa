Our Genesis

A Legacy Carved in
Botanical Truth

Founded by Elena Voss in a small coastal atelier, JOFA began with a single mission: to prove that nature's untamed potency exceeds synthetic alternatives.

For three decades, we have remained independent, sourcing rare minerals from the deep onyx mines and antioxidants from centuries-old olive groves. This history is not just our past—it is the blueprint for every ritual we craft today.
